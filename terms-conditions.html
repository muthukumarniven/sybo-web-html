1. DESCRIPTION OF Sybo.ai

Sybo.ai is conceived as a supportive platform for users seeking assistance with emotional challenges, stresses, and issues they encounter in their daily lives. Our app utilizes advanced AI technology to facilitate conversational interactions through an avatar, aiming to provide users with personalized, insightful guidance.

Through engaging video conversations, the avatar in Sybo.ai initiates dialogues by posing a series of thoughtful questions. Users respond verbally, allowing the AI system to gauge and understand their specific situations. The essence of Sybo.ai is to deliver AI-powered suggestions based on the context shared by users, offering prospective pathways to address their challenges.

The primary intention of Sybo.ai is not to replace professional advice but to act as an adjunctive tool that complements traditional support systems. The recommendations and insights generated by our AI should be perceived as initial guidance, encouraging users to explore further with qualified professionals where necessary.

Beyond providing guidance, Sybo.ai is committed to privacy and security. Every conversation held within the app is securely recorded and stored, accessible solely to the user for replay and personal reflection. This commitment ensures that users can engage with the app confidently, knowing that their interactions remain private and confidential.

Sybo.ai also offers flexible usage options, permitting users to initiate new sessions or continue from previous ones, thus fostering an environment of continuous growth and learning. By allowing users to revisit past sessions, the app encourages a deeper understanding of personal progress over time.

Through a subscription-based model, Sybo.ai grants users ongoing access to its interactive support system, with scalable plans designed to accommodate varying levels of use. This structured approach makes emotional and psychological support accessible and convenient, meeting users where they are in their journey towards well-being.
